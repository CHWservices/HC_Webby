Our exciting algorithmic meeting...

Once upon a time, in the midst of our favorite pandemic (yes, that one), Holly and Charlie's love story began in the most modern way possible: online. Sparks flew across the digital divide, leaving many to wonder how the algorithms managed to connect someone from Warwickshire with someone from London. But, as they say, love finds a way—or in this case, a Wi-Fi signal.

Their first in-person meeting was at the bustling Marylebone station. What started as a simple plan for a walk and a coffee quickly escalated to a drink, and then to a full-blown dinner. Clearly, they were off to a running start!

Since that fateful day, Holly and Charlie have become quite the globetrotters. They've explored:

  • The scenic landscapes of Cornwall and Wales
  • Soaked up the sun in Spain
  • Delved into the rich history of Poland and Italy
  • Ventured down under to Australia
  • Enjoyed the quaint charm of the Isle of Wight

Their family expanded too, with the addition of little Elton. Well, "little" might be a stretch—Elton is a bundle of joy and fur, spreading love (and shedding) wherever he goes.


In true adventurous spirit, Holly and Charlie have navigated new jobs, new homes, and even new counties together, often all at once. Because, naturally, doing everything simultaneously is the easiest way, right?


As they continue to grow together, Charlie is learning the fine art of punctuality, while Holly is slowly coming to terms with the fact that he might never actually master it. But hey, love is all about compromise!


And so, their journey continues, filled with laughter, love, and the occasional late arrival.

Here's to Holly and Charlie, a couple whose love story is as unique and wonderful as they are.
